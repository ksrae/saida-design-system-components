# Flex Align
## Definition
The `align` attribute defines the alignment of the flex container's children along the cross axis.

## Values
- start(Default): Items are aligned at the start of the cross axis.
- end: Items are aligned at the end of the cross axis.
- center: Items are aligned at the center of the cross axis.
- stretch: Items are stretched to fill the container.
- baseLine: Items are aligned at the baseline of the cross axis.
