# Typography 

## Definition 
Typography serves as the foundation of our design system as it establishes visual hierarchy, increases readability, strengthens brand identity, and ensures consistency across different platforms and devices.<br/><br/>
The default alignment of DC component fonts is Baseline.

<a href="https://www.figma.com/design/SGdtLbL9A4uZbVVJr0Uesh/Foundation?node-id=144213-2752&m=dev" target="_blank">Typography Guide in Figma</a>

## Typefaces - Roboto
Roboto was chosen as the product's primary font for its clean, modern look, excellent readability on a variety of screen sizes, seamless integration with digital interfaces, and improved overall user experience.<br/>
This Roboto font family is also a typeface selected in our brand identity guide.
