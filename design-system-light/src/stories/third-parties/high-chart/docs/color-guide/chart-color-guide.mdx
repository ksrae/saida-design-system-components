## Overview 
Data visualization is the representation of information in pictorial or graphical format, such as charts, graphs, maps, and diagrams. <br/>
These visuals aid in the communication of complex data so that insights can be more easily drawn.
Because color can affect our perception of information, the appropriate use of color is critical in making a data visualization successful.<br/>

## Status

<b>--charts-status-information :</b> For data visualization communicating low priority or in-progress statuses. <br/>
<b>--charts-status-warning :</b> For data visualization communicating caution, such as 'at risk' or 'medium priority'.<br/> 
<b>--charts-status-danger :</b> For data visualization communicating negative or critical information, such as 'off track' or 'high priority'.<br/> 
<b>--charts-status-discovery :</b> For data visualization communicating 'new' statuses.<br/> 
<b>--charts-status-neutral :</b> For data visualization communicating 'to-do' statuses.<br/> 
<b>--charts-status-success :</b> For data visualization communicating positive information, such as 'on track' or done.
